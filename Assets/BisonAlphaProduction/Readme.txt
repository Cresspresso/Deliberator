
Deliberator

by Team Bison

2020

Controls:
- Normal FPS controls:
	- Mouse to look around.
	- WASD to walk.
	- Space to jump.
- Left Mouse Button to interact with doors, key cards, etc.
- While holding a key card:
	- Left Mouse Button to use it on a Lock.
- R to restart the level.
- K to pause/unpause the time limit.
